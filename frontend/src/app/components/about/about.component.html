Where t‑shirt creators meet their people

grit:Tee is an online marketplace focused entirely on t‑shirts. It connects independent sellers, small brands, and designers with shoppers who love unique designs inspired by coding, anime, and internet culture.

The platform is built to make it simple for creators to open a shop, share their ideas, and reach fans who actually understand their references—without getting lost in a generic, oversaturated catalog.

For Shoppers

Discover tees that match your hobbies, fandoms, and everyday personality.

Whether you are into developer jokes, anime storylines, or subtle references only your friends will get, grit:Tee helps you find t‑shirts that feel personal, not generic.

  • Browse t‑shirts by category, fandom, style, or creator.
  • See clear product details and learn about each seller’s story.
  • Support independent creators and small brands with every purchase.

grit:Tee helps t‑shirt creators, artists, and small brands launch and grow their own mini store inside a focused marketplace.

  • Open your seller shop in a few guided steps.
  • Upload designs, set pricing, and manage inventory in one place.
  • Showcase your brand with a dedicated seller profile.
  • Reach communities that love niche, story-driven t‑shirt designs.

For Sellers

Fair visibility and a space where your designs are the main event, not an afterthought.
